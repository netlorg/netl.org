netl.org

netl was my first open-source project. Well, sort of. I mean I released it as open-source, and at least one obsecure Linux distribution added it to their repository. netl was one of the first network sniffers to detect stealth scans by nmap which was being developed on the same university residential networks. However, nobody other than myself actually worked on the project (to me, the mark of a successful open source project is at least a few contributors), and even if netl did compile on modern Linux, there are better alternatives today, like wireshark. Still, this little project has a soft spot in my heart for being the first thing that "put out there". So. I am keeping the domain. For now.
